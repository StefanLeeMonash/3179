Percentage of those who sought treatment by Gender between 2016 and 2018

It can be seen that Men typically sought treatment the least while those who identified as LGBTQI+ sought treatment the most. Over the three years the proportion of individuals from each gender that sought out treatment remained relatively similar, with a spike to 94% during 2017 for those who identified as LGBTQI+. Moreover, it followed a similar trend with those who identified as LGBTQI+ seeking treatment the most, followed by Women and lastly Men.
